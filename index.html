Types Of AI

Getting to Know the Types of Artificial Intelligence Technology

Narrow AI

Narrow AI also known as weak AI is a type of AI designed to perform certain tasks that are specific in nature. This AI technology has one reliable cognitive ability. However, narrow AI does not have the ability to learn independently.

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)

there is AGI or commonly known as general AI or strong AI. This AI technology has the ability to learn, think, and perform various actions like humans. Its design aims to create machines that can perform various tasks and help with daily work.

Artificial Superintelligence

There is also Artificial Superintelligence (ASI) or super AI. Its existence is still in the realm of science fiction which is depicted as having its own level of consciousness and having high knowledge beyond human capabilities. This technology is depicted in various films, including Terminator, as a technology that will dominate humans.

About

AI (Artificial Intelligence)

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a field of science that focuses on developing machines and computers that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. AI is a broad field that includes many disciplines, such as computer science, mathematics, neuroscience, linguistics, philosophy, and psychology.
